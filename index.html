About Funalytics

Smart mutual fund dashboard

Analyze, compare, and plan investments with clarity.

What is Funalytics

Funalytics turns mutual fund data into clean rankings, performance insights, comparison tools, and investment planning flows so decisions feel structured instead of noisy.

How Funalytics Score Works

RReturns

Rewards funds that deliver stronger 1Y, 3Y, and 5Y return profiles.

CConsistency

Looks at stability, volatility, and risk-adjusted performance signals.

PRelative Performance

Ranks each fund against its own category for a fairer comparison.

Investment Planner

The planner uses your monthly amount, time horizon, risk profile, and expected return to estimate projected wealth, contribution value, and a category-wise portfolio allocation.
